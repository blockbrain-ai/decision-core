# Anti-Money Laundering Policy

## Transaction Monitoring

### Thresholds

All transactions exceeding $10,000 must be reported to the compliance team within 24 hours.

If the cumulative amount exceeds $25,000 in a 30-day period, the account must be flagged for enhanced review.

Transactions below $500 may be processed without additional checks.

### Obligations

- Staff must verify customer identity before processing any international transfer.
- All suspicious activity must be documented with evidence of the investigation outcome.
- Managers shall review flagged transactions within 2 business days.

### Prohibitions

Processing transactions from sanctioned jurisdictions is forbidden.

Staff must not override automated holds without dual authorization.

Accounts shall not be closed without completing the mandatory exit review.

## Customer Due Diligence

### Identity Verification

| Requirement | Threshold | Action |
|------------|-----------|--------|
| Basic ID check | All customers | Verify government-issued ID |
| Enhanced due diligence | High-risk customers | Obtain source of funds documentation |
| Ongoing monitoring | PEP accounts | Quarterly review required |

### Evidence Requirements

Evidence of identity verification must be retained for a minimum of 7 years.

Proof of source of funds is required for all transactions exceeding $50,000.

Documentation required for any account opened for a foreign national.

### Approval Requirements

New accounts for politically exposed persons must be approved by the Head of Compliance.

Approval required for any exception to the standard due diligence process.

Changes to customer risk rating must be approved by a senior compliance officer.

## Human Oversight

### Decision Review

All automated decisions affecting customer access must have human oversight within 48 hours.

A human reviewer is required for any decision that results in account termination.

### Protected Attributes

Customer decisions must not consider race, religion, or national origin as factors.

Age-based restrictions are prohibited except where required by law.

## Routing Rules

### Escalation Paths

High-value decisions shall be routed to the senior review panel.

Decisions involving protected categories must be routed to the ethics committee.
